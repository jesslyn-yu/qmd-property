About QMD Property

We exist to make property investing measurable, transparent and profitable.

Founded in Sydney, QMD Property was built on a simple belief: investors deserve advice grounded in numbers, not noise.

Our Story

From spreadsheets to a full-service agency

QMD Property started in 2014 when our founders — an agent and a property analyst — kept seeing the same problem: investors were buying on emotion and marketing gloss, then wondering why their returns disappointed.

They began modelling every deal on yield, cap rate and long-term growth before a single inspection. Word spread. What began as a side practice for a handful of clients grew into a full-service Sydney agency spanning acquisition, sales and property management.

Today QMD has guided more than 1,200 investors and overseen upwards of $480M in property transactions across Greater Sydney. The discipline hasn't changed: we put the numbers first, every time, and we only recommend what we'd buy ourselves.
